Chapter One

Kota

Kota carries a calm that is hard to find elsewhere — a city that balances ambition and peace in perfect harmony. The evenings are golden, the roads are breezy, and the vibe is uniquely its own.

I spent countless evenings wandering through the city, soaking in its quiet energy and finding beauty in its simplest corners ✨

❤️ Best Memory

Enjoying peaceful city lights and warm evening walks through Kota's streets.

🌆 City Lights 🍽️ Local Food 🌙 Evening Vibes

Uttar Pradesh
Chapter Two

Varanasi

There is something about Varanasi that touches your soul before you even set foot on its ancient ghats. It is alive with centuries of devotion, sound, colour and prayer.

Watching the Ganga Aarti as the lamps reflected on the river at dusk was one of the most breathtaking moments of my life 🌊

❤️ Best Memory

The golden glow of Ganga Aarti lamps dancing across the sacred river at sunset.

🪔 Ganga Aarti ⛵ Boat Rides 🛕 Ancient Temples
03
Uttar Pradesh
Chapter Three

Prayagraj

Prayagraj is where three rivers meet — and where calm meets culture. The city has an energy that is both serene and vibrant, with beautiful horizons at every turn.

Sitting by the water as the sky turned amber at sunset felt like the world slowing down, just for a moment 🌅

❤️ Best Memory

Watching the sky melt into shades of orange and gold across the open horizon.

🌊 Triveni Sangam 🌅 Sunsets 🌿 Nature Walks
